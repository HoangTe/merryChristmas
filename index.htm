We love Christmas

Christmas is one of the major festivals celebrated by the people across the globe. Everyone enjoys a cultural holiday on this day. It is celebrated every year on 25th of December. It is also known as the Feast day of Christ and celebrated in the honour of birth of Jesus Christ. People visit church and offer prayers to the lord on this day. Christmas is the day of great significance and joy for the Christian community.
There is also a tradition of giving gifts like sweets, chocolates, greeting cards, Christmas trees, decorative items etc to the family members, friends, relatives and neighbours. People start Christmas preparations well in advance with great enthusiasm and joy.
